Privacy Policy

Squish Merge · Last updated: July 8, 2026

Squish Merge is designed to be privacy-friendly. In the current web version:

  • High scores, collection progress, and settings are stored only on your device (browser local storage).
  • We do not require an account.
  • We do not intentionally collect personal information.
  • No analytics or ad SDKs are bundled in this open web build by default.

If a future App Store / Google Play build adds ads, analytics, or cloud saves, this policy will be updated before those features ship, and in-app consent will be requested where required (e.g. GDPR / ATT).
